Example Stallion Clickbank Affiliate Links

Replace CLICKBANKUSERNAME with your Clickbank Username
Replace domain.com with your domainname.

Text Link
---------

<a href="http://CLICKBANKUSERNAME.stallionad.hop.clickbank.net/?tid=STALLION&dpdid=Stallion_seo">Stallion SEO Ad Theme</a>

The affiliate link above is built into the Stallion theme
as a widget called "Stallion Affiliate Banner". Add your
Clickbank username to the main Stallion options page and
the built in banner ad will automatically add your Clickbank
username (you will recieve a share of Stallion sales from your site).
